Abbvie @ Research Park

Who We Are

AbbVie (NYSE:ABBV) is a global, research-based biopharmaceutical company formed in 2013 following separation from Abbott Laboratories. The company's mission is to use its expertise, dedicated people and unique approach to innovation to develop and market advanced therapies that address some of the world's most complex and serious diseases. AbbVie employs approximately 25,000 people worldwide and markets medicines in more than 170 countries. The AbbVie Innovation Center is located in the Research Park at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and provides an opportunity for students and AbbVie's Research and Development IT groups to collaborate on projects to help advance our science. The office currently has over 50 dedicated interns and is the fastest growing in Research Park.

What We Do

The AbbVie Innovation Center has three main functions: identify, ideate, and resolve. Problems identified in corporate that need a fresh set of eyes are often sent to the Innovation Center for a different, more out of the box approach. Students work in sub-teams to advance results for different aspects of the company, providing anything from IT support to cross-platform software scaliblity. Teams work to apply emerging technologies towards the growth of AbbVie as a whole, providing creative solutions for both short and long-term problems.

How We Are Unique

We pride ourselves on being a part of the University of Illinois community. All employees at the Innovation Center have a background stemming from the University of Illinois, and all interns are current students at the University. Being a part of such a community allows for access to University resources, along with a student workforce that can apply in-classroom concepts directly towards real-life problems. The University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign is nationally recognized for having top-tier business and engineering programs allowing for Abbvie to source talented and high capability individuals.
